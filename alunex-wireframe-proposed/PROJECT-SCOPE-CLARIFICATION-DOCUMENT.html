Client Asked: "in enginering i c shop drawing materail submission RFI structural material take off fabrication coordination earlier currently its blank how this functions can u explan are its a step by step process"

Answer: Great question! The Engineering module functions are part of the construction project workflow. Here's how they work:

Engineering Module Workflow (Step-by-step):

  1. Shop Drawings: Create and manage technical drawings for fabrication
    • Upload drawing files (PDF, DWG, etc.)
    • Track revision numbers and dates
    • Get client approval on each drawing
    • Status: Draft ‚Üí Submitted ‚Üí Approved/Rejected ‚Üí Revised
  2. Material Submission: Submit material specifications for approval
    • List material type, brand, specifications
    • Upload product datasheets and certificates
    • Track approval status from client/consultant
  3. RFI (Request for Information): Ask questions to client/consultant
    • Create RFI with question details
    • Assign to specific person for response
    • Track response time and answers
    • Link RFIs to specific drawings or project areas
  4. Structural Analysis: Engineering calculations and reports
    • Upload structural calculation documents
    • Track approval from structural engineer
    • Link to specific drawings
  5. Material Take-off: Calculate quantities needed
    • List all materials with quantities
    • Link to BOQ (Bill of Quantities)
    • Update as design changes
  6. Fabrication Coordination: Coordinate with fabrication team
    • Share approved drawings with fabrication
    • Track fabrication progress
    • Manage any changes or issues

Yes, they follow a step-by-step process:
Design ‚Üí Shop Drawings ‚Üí Material Submission ‚Üí Approvals ‚Üí Material Take-off ‚Üí Fabrication ‚Üí Installation

What is a Wireframe vs Functional System?

Important Distinction:

Wireframe (What you have now, partially): Visual mockup showing what the page will look like. You can click and navigate, but no real data is saved. It's like looking at a picture of a house.

Functional System (What was originally ordered): Working application where you can actually enter data, save it, retrieve it, and use it for your real business. It's like actually living in the house.
