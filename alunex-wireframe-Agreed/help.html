Frequently Asked Questions

How do I invite external team members with Gmail or Outlook?
Go to Settings → Team Members → Click "Invite Member". You can invite ANY email address including Gmail (@gmail.com), Outlook (@outlook.com), Yahoo, or corporate emails. They'll receive an invitation link via email to create their account.
Does file storage use my server space?
No! Files are automatically synced with your Google Drive account, so you only use your Google Drive storage (15GB free). For large files stored elsewhere (OneDrive, Dropbox, CAD servers), use the "Add Link" feature to save links without using any storage.
How do I allocate budgets to different departments?
Go to Departments & Budget → Select your project → Click "Adjust Budgets". Enter your total project value and profit margin. The system calculates your working budget, then you can allocate specific amounts to Engineering, Procurement, Fabrication, and Installation departments.
Can I filter the dashboard by specific projects?
Yes! At the top right of the Dashboard page, use the "View Projects" dropdown to select individual projects, all projects, or enable multi-select mode to view multiple projects at once. All metrics and task lists update based on your selection.
